Night Packages

Night Owl Package: For the true night adventurer, this package takes you on an all-night journey. Beginning at 10:00 pm, you’ll visit three of Accra’s top nightlife venues with your private ride and escort (upon request). Enjoy exclusive access and reserved tables. The night concludes at 4:00 am. ( Note:This package is intense and recommended for those who enjoy late-night experiences.)

Twilight Package: Begin your evening at 5:00 pm, watching a gorgeous sunset. Your private ride and guide will take you to two popular venues after enjoying sunset drinks. This relaxed evening ends at 11:00 pm.

Dine-out Package Indulge in a gourmet dinner at one of Accra’s finest restaurants, with a live African music performance setting the mood. Afterward, relax in a stylish lounge. This elegant night starts at 6:00 pm and wraps up at 9:00 pm.

Safari Packages

Mole National Park Safari: Embark on a safari in Ghana’s largest wildlife refuge. Start with a morning game drive, spotting elephants, antelopes, and warthogs, followed by lunch and an afternoon walking safari, guided by a local ranger. Experience the untamed wilderness in the heart of Ghana.

Northern Savannah Expedition: This journey takes you to the Paga Crocodile Pond and the Wechiau Hippo Sanctuary. A canoe safari on the River offers a chance to observe hippos and other wildlife in their natural environment.

Birdwatching & Nature Walk: Immerse yourself in the natural beauty of Ghana with a day of birdwatching and wildlife exploration. Whether you're visiting lush forests, savannah reserves, or tranquil conservation areas, you'll have the chance to spot rare and colorful bird species, alongside other wildlife like baboons and antelopes. This experience offers a peaceful connection to nature, perfect for bird enthusiasts and those seeking a deeper appreciation of Ghana's rich biodiversity. Tailor your adventure based on your preferred location and pace, making it an unforgettable journey into the wild.

Historical Packages

Accra Half-Day Tour: A compact but insightful tour from 9:00 am to 1:00 pm. Visit iconic landmarks like Black Star Square and the Kwame Nkrumah Memorial Park.

Ghana Forts Tour: Explore the historic forts of Cape Coast, which played pivotal roles in Ghana’s past. This tour gives you a deeper understanding of the nation’s journey through time.

Accra Food Tour: Experience Ghanaian culture through its cuisine. This guided tour takes you through Accra’s streets to sample traditional dishes, uncovering the culinary history behind each.

Adventure Packages

Volta Region Adventure: A breathtaking drive takes you to the majestic Wli Waterfalls, the tallest in West Africa. Embark on a guided hike through the Agumatsa Wildlife Sanctuary before enjoying a relaxing boat ride on the Volta River. End your day with an authentic Ghanaian meal at a local restaurant.

Mountain Climbing & Canopy Walk: Kick-off with a thrilling ascent, conquering one of Ghana’s scenic mountain peaks. The climb offers a challenging yet rewarding experience, with breathtaking views awaiting you at the summit. Embark on an exhilarating canopy walk through a tropical rainforest, where you’ll traverse suspended bridges high above the forest floor, offering a unique perspective of the lush greenery below. This adventure perfectly blends physical challenge with awe-inspiring natural beauty, making it ideal for thrill-seekers and nature lovers alike.

Water Sports Extravaganza: Spend an exhilarating day by the water. Dive into a variety of exciting water activities, including jet skiing, kayaking, paddleboarding, or even windsurfing. Whether you're seeking high-energy thrills or more relaxed water adventures, this experience is tailored to suit your preferences. After an action-packed day, unwind on the sandy shores and indulge in a fresh seafood feast, enjoying the coastal breeze and stunning views.
